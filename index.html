About

Aghamore welcomes you !



Aghamore Tidy Village is all about, well Aghamore, and keeping it looking great and clean so that everyone in the community and visitors alike can enjoy spending time here!


Check out the 'Discover' section to see any upcoming events we have planned and view our gallery.


We are always on the lookout for more volunteers to help with never ending list of jobs and ideas to get done. If you feel you want to help get in touch via our contact page, or just provide us with feedback or ideas you think might be helpful!!
